About me

After 3

After 3 is a band consisting of myself, Justice Lee (ukulele player in video), Shuacua Moua (piano player in video), and Teddy Chaleunsouk (not shown in video). I play ukulele, produces beats, and write lyrics. Shuacua write lyrics, produces beats, plays piano, and sings. Teddy writes lyrics, produces beats, plays guitar, bass, drums, and dabbles with the ukulele. We make a range of music consisting of lofi to actual song compositions.
